Air Conditioning

The A/C is divided into two sides, known as the high and low side. On the high side, the compressor functions like a pump, moving refrigerant hrough the sstem. On the low side, the expansion valve restricts refrigerant flow, lowering the pressure and the temperature.

Radiator

The radiator acts as art of the vehicle's cooling system, circulating antifreeze/coolant through a series of tubes towards the core of the engine. Air passes through the cooling fins surrounding the core, releasing heat onto the fins to dissipate the heat. This is necessary to prevent the vehicle from overheating.
